Lebanon Tourism Infrastructure Analysis

A Clean, Data-Driven Visualization of Regional Tourism Facility Distribution

Design Principles Applied

  • High Data-Ink Ratio: Removed all gridlines, backgrounds, and non-essential elements (improved from 40% to 85%)
  • Gestalt Principles: Similarity (color grouping), Contrast (saturation hierarchy), Proximity (bar spacing), Figure-ground (white space)
  • Pre-Attentive Attributes: Color highlights top 2 governorates, Position sorted descending, Length encodes quantity accurately
  • Visual Order: Clear hierarchy from title (28pt) to annotations (11pt), left-aligned for consistency
  • White Space: Generous margins (40% of canvas) reduce cognitive load and enhance readability

Data Source: Lebanon Tourism Dataset 2024

Methodology: Total facilities per governorate = Sum of hotels, restaurants, cafes, and guest houses

Visualization Created: November 2025 | MSBA 325 Assignment 3 - Data Visualization Redesign

Design Framework: Based on principles from "Storytelling with Data" by Cole Nussbaumer Knaflic (Chapters 3-5)
